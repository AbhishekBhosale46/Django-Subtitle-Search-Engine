Subtitle Search Engine - uploading, processing, and searching videos effortlessly. Using the power of ccextractor binary, we extract subtitles directly from your uploaded videos, ensuring a seamless experience without relying on external APIs.

Our backend, built on the robust Django framework, handles video processing as a background task using Celery workers, making the entire process efficient and user-friendly. Once processed, videos are securely stored in Amazon S3, guaranteeing reliability and scalability.

The system indexes the extracted subtitles into DynamoDB, allowing you to search for specific words or phrases within the video content. Imagine uploading a music video, and with a simple search, discovering the exact time segments where your chosen phrases are mentioned.
